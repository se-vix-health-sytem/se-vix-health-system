Detailed Description

Public-facing site controllers — no authentication required.

These pages are accessible to anyone and serve as the hospital's public information portal.

  • HomePageController — landing page (/)
  • AboutController — about the hospital (/about)
  • ContactController — contact form (/contact)
  • DepartmentController — department list and detail pages
  • SpecialistController — specialist listings and profiles
  • MapController — hospital locations and directions
  • QuestionnaireController— patient intake questionnaire
  • LegalController — privacy policy, GDPR, terms, accessibility

All routes in this package are listed under permitAll() in vixhealthsystem.config.SecurityConfig.
