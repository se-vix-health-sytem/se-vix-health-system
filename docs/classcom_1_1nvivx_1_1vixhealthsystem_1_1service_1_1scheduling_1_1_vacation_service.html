Detailed Description

Manages employee vacation requests from creation through approval/denial (UC29, FR6.2).

Requests are stored in vacations.json via JsonVacationRepository rather than the relational database, keeping vacation data separate from the core HR schema. The staff manager is the only actor who can add, approve, deny, or delete requests — employees themselves cannot self-serve vacation through this system.
