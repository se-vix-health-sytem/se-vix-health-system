Detailed Description

Controllers for hospital staff — one controller per role, plus shared utilities.

Each staff role has its own controller and dashboard template. The AuthController handles the shared staff login/logout flow (Firebase-backed). EmployeeResourceController is shared across all roles — any employee can take consumable resources from storage.

  • AuthController — staff login/logout (/login)
  • MedicalSpecialistController — calendar, schedule, appointments, surgeries, prescriptions (/medical-specialist)
  • SecretaryController — patient search, appointment management, room overview (/secretary)
  • BuyerController — inventory management, purchasing (/buyer)
  • TechnicianController — machinery monitoring and maintenance (/technician)
  • StaffManagerController — employee management, shifts, vacations, audit logs (/staff-manager)
  • EmployeeResourceController — resource-take flow shared by all staff (/employee/resources)
