Detailed Description

External integration services — Firebase, payments, notifications, questionnaire.

Everything here talks to something outside the core domain:

  • FirebaseAuthService — creates, deletes, and authenticates Firebase Authentication accounts for employees and patients
  • PaymentService — simulates a payment gateway (demo/prototype only; no real charges are made)
  • NotificationService — logs simulated email notifications (demo mode; no actual mail is sent)
  • QuestionnaireService — processes patient intake questionnaires and returns a triage recommendation
