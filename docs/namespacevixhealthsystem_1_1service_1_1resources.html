Detailed Description

Resource management services: inventory, machinery, and room availability.

The split between InventoryService (consumables) and MachineryService (equipment) reflects a real operational distinction; consumables are taken and replenished while machinery is tracked by status transitions and maintenance history.

  • InventoryService - stock levels; buyers add, employees take; both actions are audit-logged (UC25, UC27)
  • MachineryService - machine status tracking and maintenance history
  • RoomService - bed availability queries across departments
  • ResourceTakeLogStore - in-memory take log for the staff manager's view
