Detailed Description

Controller for public legal and compliance pages — no base URL prefix.

Serves the static legal documents required by Italian and EU law: privacy policy, GDPR notice, terms of use, and accessibility statement. Each handler simply routes to the corresponding Thymeleaf fragment; no dynamic data is needed.
