Detailed Description

Controller for Secretary staff members — base URL /secretary.

Secretaries handle the administrative side of patient flow and appointment management. This controller covers room management (UC21), patient admission and dismissal (UC22, UC23), appointment booking/confirmation/cancellation/ rescheduling (UC14, UC15, UC16), patient search, and the secretary's own shift and profile pages.

Only accessible to users with ROLE_SECRETARY.

GET /secretary/dashboard — render the secretary's overview dashboard.

Resolves the secretary's department name by reloading a fresh entity from the database (avoiding lazy-loading failures on the detached session entity). Populates room and appointment summary counts.
