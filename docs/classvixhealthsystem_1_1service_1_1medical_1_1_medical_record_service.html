Detailed Description

Manages patient medical records : creation and appending of diagnoses, prescriptions, and exam results.

Annotated @Transactional(readOnly=true) at the class level; write methods override with @Transactional.

All mutations are recorded via AuditService to satisfy NFR02 (traceability). When a patient has no record yet, write operations auto-create one rather than failing.

Prescription issuance delegates to vixhealthsystem.model.person.employee.MedicalSpecialist#appPrescriptionForPatient so that domain invariants are enforced by the model layer.
