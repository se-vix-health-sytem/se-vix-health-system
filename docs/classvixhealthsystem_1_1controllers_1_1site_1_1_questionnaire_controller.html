Detailed Description

Controller for the public symptom-triage questionnaire : base URL /questionnaire.

Guides prospective patients through a lightweight symptom assessment and recommends the appropriate specialist or department. The mapping from symptom area to specialist type, and the urgency calculation, are both handled in-process without any external service dependency, keeping the feature usable even when the database is unavailable.
