Detailed Description

Clinical domain: everything that happens to a patient medically.

MedicalRecord is the aggregate root here. Prescriptions, surgeries, and conditions should always be added through its domain methods (addPrescription, addSurgery) to keep bidirectional associations consistent. Don't set the collections directly.

The other classes in this package are owned by the record: Appointment connects a patient to a specialist at a given time; MedicalCondition stores diagnoses and exam results; Prescription holds medication orders; Surgery ties a procedure to both the patient and the operating specialist.
