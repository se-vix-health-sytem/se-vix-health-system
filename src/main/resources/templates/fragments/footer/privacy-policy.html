Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

VIX Health System is committed to protecting your personal and medical data in compliance with EU GDPR (Regulation 2016/679).

1. Data Collection

We collect only necessary personal data required for medical services, appointment booking, and patient identification. This includes:

  • Name, surname, and fiscal code
  • Contact information (email, phone number)
  • Date and place of birth
  • Medical history, diagnoses, prescriptions, and exam results
  • Appointment history and payment records
2. Data Access Control (NFR01, NFR05)

Only authorized personnel (doctors, nurses, administrative staff) can access patient data. All access is authenticated and logged. Role-based access control ensures that each employee sees only the data necessary for their job function.

3. Session Security

Users are automatically logged out after 8 minutes of inactivity for security reasons. All passwords are hashed and stored securely using industry-standard encryption.

4. Data Encryption (NFR09)

All medical records are stored in encrypted form and require authentication to access. Data transmission between client and server uses TLS 1.2+ encryption.

5. Data Retention

Medical data is stored for at least 5 years as required by Italian health regulations (NFR09). Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the provision of medical services and legal compliance.

6. Your Rights (NFR04)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: Object to certain data processing activities
7. How to Exercise Your Rights

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics without explicit consent.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
