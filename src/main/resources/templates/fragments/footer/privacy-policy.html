Privacy Policy

VIX Health System is committed to protecting your personal and medical data in compliance with EU GDPR (Regulation 2016/679).

Data Collection

We collect only necessary personal data required for medical services, appointment booking, and patient identification.

Data Access Control (NFR01, NFR05)

Only authorized personnel (doctors, nurses, administrative staff) can access patient data. All access is authenticated and logged.

Session Security

Users are automatically logged out after 8 minutes of inactivity for security reasons.

Data Encryption (NFR09)

All medical records are stored in encrypted form and require authentication to access.

Data Retention

Medical data is stored for at least 5 years as required by Italian health regulations.

User Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion (within 30 days)
  • Export personal data
